About

BTS also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. Was known for their lyrics that often focused on personal and social commentary, touch on the themes of mental health, troubles of school-age youth and coming of age, loss, the journey towards loving oneself, and individualism.The group members consist of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.

Early Years

BTS was formed in 2010 after Big Hit Entertainment CEO Bang Si-hyuk met RM and was impressed by his rapping skills. BTS was originally supposed to be a hip hop group but soon after the group was created, Bang Si-hyuk decided to create an idol group. On June 12, 2013, BTS debuted with their first single album 2 Cool 4 Skool.
